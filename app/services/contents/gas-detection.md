## Gas & Flame Detection Systems

We provide fixed gas and flame detection systems designed to **continuously monitor hazardous environments** and trigger alarms before incidents escalate.

### Detection Capabilities
- Flammable gases
- Toxic gases
- Oxygen depletion
- UV & IR flame detection

### Integration
Systems are fully integrated with:
- Fire alarm panels
- Shutdown systems
- Fire suppression systems

### Key Advantages
- Early hazard detection
- Automated response
- Improved personnel safety
